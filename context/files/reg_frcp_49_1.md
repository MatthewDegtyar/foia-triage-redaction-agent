# Fed. R. Crim. P. 49.1 — Privacy protection in criminal filings
The criminal-case analog to Civil Rule 5.2. Redact to: SSN/taxpayer-ID last four digits; birth year only; a minor's initials; financial-account last four; and the **city and state only** of a home address. Additional caution for cooperating defendants and witnesses whose identification could endanger safety (overlaps b7(F)).
