# Fed. R. Civ. P. 5.2 — Privacy protection for court filings
Unless the court orders otherwise, a filing that contains these must be redacted to:
- **Social-Security / taxpayer-ID numbers** — last four digits only.
- **Birth dates** — year only.
- **Names of minors** — initials only.
- **Financial-account numbers** — last four digits only.
In criminal cases, **home addresses** are additionally limited to the city and state. The responsibility to redact rests on the filer, not the clerk; an unredacted, unsealed filing waives protection of the filer's OWN information only.
