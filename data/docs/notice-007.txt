PUBLIC NOTICE — Cedar Park Planning Commission

The Cedar Park Planning Commission will hold a public hearing on the Cedar Park solar permit
program (case CP-SOLAR-2231) on March 14, 2026 at 6:00 PM in the Council Chambers, 450 Cypress
Creek Road. The agenda, staff summary, and decision record are available on the city website.
Members of the public may submit written comment to the Commission clerk in advance of the
hearing. This notice is issued in accordance with the Open Meetings Act.
